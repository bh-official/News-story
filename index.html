City Council Approves New Public Park Project

Author: Bhuvaneswari Babu
Date: 5 January 2026

The city council has approved plans for a new public park to improve green spaces and encourage outdoor activities for residents. The project aims to support community wellbeing and environmental sustainability.

The new park will feature walking paths, children’s play areas, and seating spaces. Construction is expected to begin later this year after final planning approvals.

Events leading to the approval

  • Proposal submitted to the council
  • Public feedback collected
  • Final approval granted
